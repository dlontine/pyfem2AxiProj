4.1. Overview¶

pyfem2 provides several elements in one- and two-dimensions.

4.1.1. Heat transfer elements¶

4.1.1.1. DiffusiveHeatTransfer2D3¶

The DiffusiveHeatTransfer2D3 element is a three-node plane element that is appropriate for diffusive heat transfer problems in 2D.

  • Active degrees of freedom: T
  • Valid loads: Heat generation, Surface flux, Surface convection

4.1.2. Truss elements¶

The truss element theory is described in Elastic bar elements.

4.1.2.1. ElasticLink1D2¶

  • Active degrees of freedom: X
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads

4.1.2.2. ElasticLink2D2¶

  • Active degrees of freedom: X,Y
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads

4.1.2.3. ElasticLink3D2¶

  • Active degrees of freedom: X,Y,Z
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads

4.1.3. Beam-column elements¶

4.1.3.1. BeamColumn2D¶

  • Active degrees of freedom: X,Y,TZ
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads

4.1.4. Stress-displacement elements¶

4.1.4.1. PlaneStrainQuad4¶

  • Active degrees of freedom: X,Y
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads, Gravity loads, Distributed load, Surface load vector, Normal surface load vector, Pressure

4.1.4.2. PlaneStrainQuad4Reduced¶

Uses a reduced integration formulation.

  • Active degrees of freedom: X,Y
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads, Gravity loads, Distributed load, Surface load vector, Normal surface load vector, Pressure

4.1.4.3. PlaneStressQuad4¶

  • Active degrees of freedom: X,Y
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads, Gravity loads, Distributed load, Surface load vector, Normal surface load vector, Pressure

4.1.4.4. PlaneStrainQuad8¶

  • Active degrees of freedom: X,Y
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads, Gravity loads, Distributed load, Surface load vector, Normal surface load vector, Pressure

4.1.4.5. PlaneStressQuad8¶

  • Active degrees of freedom: X,Y
  • Valid loads: Concentrated loads, Gravity loads, Distributed load, Surface load vector, Normal surface load vector, Pressure
